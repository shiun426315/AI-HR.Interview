We Understand the Challenge

HR 主管每天都在面對的
三個現實問題

Three Problems Hiring Managers
Face Every Single Day

⏱
初篩耗費大量人力
Screening Eats Up Headcount

半導體廠職缺競爭激烈，HR 每季需篩選數百份履歷，安排電話初篩，光是排程與等待就消耗半數工作時間。

Competition for semiconductor roles is fierce. HR teams screen hundreds of resumes a quarter and schedule phone screens for each one — scheduling and waiting alone eat up half the workweek.

📋
評估標準難以一致
Evaluation Standards Don't Hold Together

不同 HR 人員對候選人的主觀印象差異大，相同條件的應徵者在不同場次可能得到截然不同的結論，影響用人決策品質。

Subjective impressions vary widely between interviewers. Two equally qualified candidates can walk away with completely different assessments, depending only on who happened to interview them.

📁
面試紀錄分散難追蹤
Interview Records Scatter Everywhere

面試筆記存在個人電腦、手寫紙本、或各式通訊軟體，主管難以彙整比較，遑論建立結構化的人才資料庫。

Notes live on personal laptops, on paper, in chat threads — nowhere consistent. Comparing candidates is hard enough; building a structured talent database is nearly impossible.

三步驟完成初篩

Three Steps to a Completed Screen

從發出連結到取得報告
全程不需 HR 人員陪同

From Sending the Link to Reading the Report —
No HR Staff Required in Between

🔗
Step 1
HR 產生專屬連結
HR Generates a Unique Link

在後台點擊一鍵產生含時效的 JWT 安全連結，複製後寄給候選人，舊連結自動失效。

One click in the backend generates a time-limited, JWT-secured link. Copy it, send it to the candidate — any prior link expires automatically.

🎙
Step 2
候選人完成語音面試
The Candidate Completes the Interview

AI HR Agent 根據職缺與履歷自然提問，最多 20 輪，同步蒐集聲學、表情與肢體行為數據。

An AI HR Agent asks questions shaped by the role and the resume — up to 20 exchanges — while collecting voice, facial, and body-language data throughout.

📊
Step 3
報告自動寫回後台
The Report Writes Itself Back

面試結束後 30 秒內，完整評估報告、逐字稿與行為信號自動整合進 HR 管理後台，供主管審核。

Within 30 seconds of the interview ending, the full evaluation — transcript, behavioral signals, and all — is already sitting in the HR backend, ready for review.

核心功能

Core Features

不只是錄音，而是
多模態行為分析

This Isn't a Recording —
It's Multimodal Behavioral Analysis

系統在候選人自然作答的過程中，同步捕捉語言與非語言訊號，提供超越傳統面試的洞察深度。

As candidates answer naturally, the system reads both what they say and how they say it — surfacing a depth of insight a traditional interview never captures.

🤖
AI 語音面試引擎
AI Voice Interview Engine

針對半導體廠職缺設計的 HR Agent，根據候選人履歷即時生成問題，語氣自然輕鬆，涵蓋品格、耐壓、離職原因、薪資期望等必問主題，並具備深入追問能力。

An HR Agent purpose-built for semiconductor roles, generating questions from each candidate's resume in a natural, conversational tone. It covers every required topic — character, stress tolerance, reason for leaving, salary expectations — and knows when to dig deeper.

繁中 / 英文雙語 Bilingual: Chinese & English 最多 20 輪對話 Up to 20 exchanges 靜音自動偵測 Automatic silence detection
📡
多模態行為信號分析
Multimodal Behavioral Signal Analysis

整合聲學分析（語速、停頓比例、填充詞）與即時表情捕捉（眼神接觸、眉頭緊皺、微笑真偽、肢體姿態），在候選人偏離基準時自動觸發 HR Agent 追問。

Combines acoustic analysis (pace, pause ratio, filler words) with real-time facial tracking (eye contact, brow tension, genuine vs. forced smiles, posture) — and triggers a follow-up automatically the moment a candidate drifts from their own baseline.

13 種情緒分類 13 emotion categories 聲學 + 表情 + 肢體 Voice + Face + Posture 相對基準比對 Compared against individual baseline
📝
結構化評估報告
Structured Evaluation Reports

面試結束後自動生成含逐字稿、行為信號彙整、整體印象、嚴重度分級警示的評估報告，並自動觸發任用建議生成，全程寫回現有後台，不需手動操作。

The moment an interview ends, the system generates a full report — transcript, consolidated behavioral signals, an overall impression, and severity-graded flags — then automatically drafts a hiring recommendation and writes the whole thing to the existing backend. No manual steps.

逐字稿 + 分析 Transcript + analysis 嚴重度分級 Severity grading 任用建議自動生成 Auto-generated hiring recommendation
🔒
企業級安全設計
Enterprise-Grade Security

JWT 時效連結防止舊連結重用，每位候選人僅能面試一次，不使用 Cookie 追蹤，音訊辨識後即丟棄，候選人資料僅存於公司後台，符合企業資安要求。

Time-limited JWT links prevent reuse — each candidate gets exactly one attempt. No cookie tracking. Audio is discarded immediately after transcription. Candidate data lives only in the company's own backend, meeting enterprise security requirements.

JWT 安全連結 JWT-secured links 不落地原始音訊 No raw audio retained CORS 白名單 CORS allowlisting
